IRON MAN

Iron Man is a brilliant inventor in advanced, high-tech armor. His suit grants him incredible strength, flight, and powerful weaponry, symbolizing both innovation and heroism.

CAPTAIN AMERICA

Captain America is a symbol of courage and patriotism, wielding an indestructible shield. With unwavering morality and strength, he stands as a beacon of hope, fighting for justice and unity.

THA HULK

The Hulk is a force of raw power and untamed strength, born from deep emotions. With his immense size and unstoppable might, he embodies both chaos and protection, a powerful guardian with a heart of compassion.

The *Venom* comic series explores the darker side of the Marvel universe, following Eddie Brock as he bonds with the alien symbiote, Venom. Together, they form an anti-hero who struggles between good and evil, with intense action, psychological battles, and moral dilemmas. The series dives deep into themes of duality, redemption, and the fine line between hero and villain, all while delivering visually striking, gritty art and thrilling storytelling.

The *Black Panther* comic series follows T'Challa, the king and protector of the technologically advanced African nation of Wakanda. As Black Panther, T'Challa combines his royal responsibilities with his superhuman abilities, drawn from the mystical Panther God. The series explores themes of legacy, identity, and power, blending rich African culture with intense action, political intrigue, and the burden of leadership. Through its vibrant storytelling, *Black Panther* showcases both heroism and the struggles of balancing tradition and modernization.
